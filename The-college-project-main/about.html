About Us

Radha Govind Group of Institutions

Radha Govind Group of Institutions, Meerut is One of the Best & Oldest Engineering Colleges of Uttar Pradesh Formerly known as Radha Govind Engineering College, it is affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU- formerly known as Uttar Pradesh Technical University). It is one of the oldest and prestigious institutions in the region, providing graduate & post graduate professional courses in the field of Engineering, Computer Applications and Management. RGGI also provides two year M.Tech programs in Civil, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication and Mechanical Engineering.

Radha Govind Group of Institutions (RGGI) is a result of sustained effort, perseverance and dedication in imparting state of the art technical education by the visionaries of Shri Krishna Shiksha Prasar Samiti (SKSPS). Registered in 1995, SKSPS has grown steadily and has successfully promoted globally competitive and value-based professional education in the country under the innovative, forward looking dynamic leadership and vision of Shri Yogesh Tyagi.

The campus of Radha Govind Engineering College (RGEC) was inspected by AICTE in the year 2010 and was granted the status of an integrated campus, which was later renamed as Radha Govind Group of Institutions.
