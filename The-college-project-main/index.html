Our facilities

Here are the facilities RGGI provide to its students..

Library

Welcome to the Library of Radha Govind Group of Institutions.The library is an integral and essential component of the institutes's education system and its objective is to nurture and develop world class students. The library is dedicated to the discovery of knowledge and to contribute to advancements in different fields of engineering and management.


Labs

Various labs are present in RGGI for the students to showcase their practical skills. Computer Labs are well equipped with six high speed IBM Xeon dual processor servers. More than 200 desktops are available all equipped with latest configurations.
Physics and chemistry labs gives students the understanding of various practical applications such as measuring wavelength of monochromatic light,polarization of light, use of polarimeter etc.
Apart from these Electrical Labs,Science Lab, OOPs Lab, Hyddraulic Labs are also present in the college.


Canteen

The canteen represents an essential part of student and campus life.The college canteen is a large spacious area with a view of greenery all around and a variety of refreshments and meals are available..
